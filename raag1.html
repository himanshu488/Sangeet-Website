Raag Kalyan

Raag Kalyan is a prominent raga in Indian classical music. It belongs to the Kalyan Thaat and is often associated with the emotions of devotion, serenity, and joy. It is traditionally performed in the evening, creating a mood of tranquility and upliftment.

Aaroh and Avroh

Aaroh: Sa Re Ga Ma' Pa Dha Ni Sa

Avroh: Sa Ni Dha Pa Ma' Ga Re Sa
